If you would like to support the work HYPE does, please donate to us using the button below. We are recognized by the IRS as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Funds collected will go towards various percussion education opportunities such as:

  • rehearsals
  • private lessons
  • master classes

  • participation in national and international competitions and festivals

  • sheet music purchases
  • insurance
  • event promotion
  • educational outreach
  • instrument and equipment repairs and purchases
